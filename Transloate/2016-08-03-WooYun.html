10 Hackers Of China’s Largest Ethical Hacking Community Arrested.
10 位中國最大白帽駭客社群的駭客被捕

10 members of China’s largest hacking group WooYun, including its founder Fang Xiaodun, were arrested last week by Chinese authorities. As this white hat group reported the security flaws in companies for free, the reasons behind the arrest are yet to be disclosed. Interestingly, the arrest was preceded by the demise of the WooYun website.

中國白帽駭客社群WooYun (烏雲)的十位成員, 包含創辦人方小頓 ( Fang Xiaodun ) 也在上禮拜被中國當局逮捕, 這白帽社群免費協助企業修補了許多關於安全的漏洞, 目前尚未公佈被捕的原因, 有趣的是, Wooyun 網站關掉是在人被捕之前

Last week, Chinese authorities arrested 10 white hat hackers of the WooYun ethical hacking group. It’s a community of ethical hackers that reports security flaws to the companies for free and helps them strengthen safeguards.

在上禮拜, 中國當局逮捕了10 位WooYun 白帽駭客社群的成員, 這個社群藉由免費回報資安漏洞, 來協助各企業加強資訊安全

The arrested members also include the founder Fang Xiaodun, according to Chinese news agency Caixinwang. With about 5,000 members, WooYun is regarded as China’s biggest white hat community.

根據中國新聞媒體 Caixinwang 表示, 這些被逮捕得成員, 是包含方小頓在內的創辦人, WooYun 有著5,000 多位成員, 是中國最大的白帽駭客群

According to media reports, the members of WooYun were arrested by the law enforcement authorities without any prior notice.

根據一些媒體報導, 這些烏雲的成員被捕執法單位之前, 事先完全沒有任何通知

“Everything happened very abruptly, even members within Wooyun were kept in the dark. People from Wooyun said there was no administrative procedures nor prior notice for the arrest.”

— said the source cited by Caixinwang

"這些事情發生的太突然了, 烏雲的很多人還不知道發生什麼事, 甚至沒有成員知道這些管理群被抓走得消息"

— 引用至Caixinwang


WooYun website is suspended indefinitely.

烏雲網站被無限期暫停

Xiaodun hasn’t shared any updates on his WeChat account since July 18. His website is also suspended indefinitely and it remains deactivated. It’s being said that WooYun has taken down the website as a precaution.

在07/18 後, 烏雲創辦人小頓沒有在他的微信 ( WeChat ) , 更新任何的更新訊息. 他的網頁關閉跟無限期暫停. 傳言 WooYun 所以把關閉網站，是做為一種預防措施

Website’s demise took place before Xiaodun’s arrest. The members of WooYun community said that Xiaodun didn’t express anything regarding pressure by the government. Xiaodun was also scheduled to deliver a talk, which has also been cancelled, at a conference in early August.

方小頓的微信帳號被關閉, 是在他被捕之前. 根據烏雲的成員表示沒有方小頓沒有任何發表關於政府給他壓力的事情, 在八月初時, 小頓預計規一場在Talk 也被取消

As nothing is certain at the moment, people are speculating that WooYun got into trouble for publicising the flaws in some websites.

目前很多事情都不確定的, 但是大家猜測, 烏雲惹上麻煩的原因次因為它share 了 " 某些網站 " 的漏洞
